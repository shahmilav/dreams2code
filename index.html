Bridging the divide, one line of code at a time.

Dreams2Code is a non-profit organization with the vision of transforming our passion for computer science into education for underserved communities. Together, we aspire to spread this passion for technology through hosting hackathons, free computing classes, and aiding local businesses with digital needs. Dreams2Code strives to empower communities, fostering a more inclusive and technologically vibrant future for all.

Our Services

Computing Classes

We teach children ages 10-14 computer science at local libraries. Students start from the basics and slowly learn how to create amazing projects using advanced programming concepts.

Helping Local Businesses

We help local businesses in the Central Jersey area with their digital needs. We create websites, mobile apps, and help with digital marketing.

Hackathons and Other Events

We plan to host local hackathons and other events to bring the community together. Please stay tuned for details.

Our Impact

Dreams2Code is a volunteer-run non-profit organization based in Plainsboro, NJ committed to enriching underserved communities through the power of technology and education. We offer comprehensive computer science education in these communities, opening doors to new opportunities and empowering residents with valuable digital skills. Our engagement with the community is further energized through organizing hackathons, which serve as dynamic platforms for learning, innovation, and cultivating a deeper interest in technology. All profits generated are reinvested into charitable causes, ensuring that our work not only uplifts communities through education and employment but also contributes directly to their well-being through philanthropy.
